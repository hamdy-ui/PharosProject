Welcome to Pharos University in Alexandria

I take pride in being the President of Pharos University, founded in 2006 as Alexandria’s first private university. Working at PUA offers constructive cooperation in a family atmosphere with a distinguished group of professors, administrative staff, and students. I work diligently to serve everyone within this giant educational institution that provides distinguished and diverse educational services. About Us

Why choose Pharos University for your future

Pharos University offers a unique blend of academic excellence, modern campus life, and strong industry connections. With international partnerships, experienced faculty, and career-focused programs, we prepare students for real-world success — both locally and globally

Discover More

The university provides a variety of academic tracks across multiple faculties. Students can choose from disciplines such as Engineering, Pharmacy, Business Administration, and Computer Science. Each program is designed with a focus on industry relevance, research opportunities, and practical application

The application process can be completed online via the official university portal. Simply visit www.universityname.edu/admissions and follow the steps provided. Applicants are required to upload documents in PDF format and pay a non-refundable application fee to complete the submission

Yes, the university offers on-campus accommodation that is equipped with essential facilities such as Wi-Fi, laundry services, and 24/7 security. Rooms are assigned based on availability, and priority is given to first-year and international students.
